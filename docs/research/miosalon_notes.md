## KEY DISCOVERY: RELATIONSHIP BETWEEN MIOSALON AND WELNS.IO

**Footer reveals:** "© copyright 2026 | Waffor Retail Solutions Private Limited | Powered by MioSalon"

**Connection confirmed:**
- Welns.io is the **consumer-facing marketplace** powered by MioSalon
- MioSalon is the **B2B salon management software**
- Both are products of **Waffor Retail Solutions Private Limited**

This represents a **two-sided platform model**:
1. **MioSalon** = Backend software for salon owners (B2B SaaS)
2. **Welns.io** = Consumer marketplace for booking (B2C marketplace)

The salons using MioSalon software can list on Welns.io for customer discovery and booking.
