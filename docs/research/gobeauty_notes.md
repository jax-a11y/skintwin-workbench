# GoBeauty.co.za Research Notes

## Overview
**Website:** https://gobeauty.co.za/
**Type:** Consumer-facing salon/spa booking marketplace (South Africa focused)
**Tagline:** "Your Favourite Salons and Spas in your pocket"
**Company:** GoBeauty Systems (Pty) Ltd. Reg. 2013/222146/07
**Location:** South Africa (phone: 021 300-2530 - Cape Town area code)

## Featured In
- News24
- DSTV
- Ventureburn
- Entrepreneur
- Grazia
- Elle

## Key Features
- Search for services by location (e.g., "manicure in sandton")
- Browse salons and spas
- Gift voucher system ("Get Gifting")
- Business listing ("Get Listed")

## Services Categories (Extensive list)
The platform covers a wide range of beauty services including:
- Massage (Swedish, Hot Stone, Deep Tissue, Aromatherapy, Reflexology, Pregnancy, Indian Head)
- Nails (Manicure, Pedicure, Gelish, Overlays)
- Hair (Keratin, Extensions, Colour, Highlights, Balayage, Blow Dry, Perm)
- Skin Treatments (Facials, Chemical Peel, Microneedling, Dermapen, Microdermabrasion, Dermaplaning)
- Aesthetic Treatments (Filler, Anti-wrinkle, HIFU, Mesotherapy, Carboxy)
- Hair Removal (Waxing, Laser, IPL, Brazilian, Threading, Sugaring)
- Brows & Lashes (Microblading, Eyelash Extensions, Tinting, Permanent Makeup)
- Body Treatments (Body Wrap, Spray Tan, Laser Lipo)
- Bridal Services

## Product Brands Represented
Extensive list of professional skincare and beauty brands:
- Environ, Nimue, Lamelle, RegimA, Dermalogica (South African favorites)
- International brands: Guinot, Elemis, Thalgo, IS Clinical, ZO Skin
- Hair brands: Kerastase, Redken, Schwarzkopf, Paul Mitchell, L'Oreal, Matrix

## Ethics Statement
"Strictly no animal nor human products"

## Business Model
- Consumer marketplace for salon/spa booking in South Africa
- Gift voucher sales
- Business listing services for salons/spas

## Significance
GoBeauty is a South African equivalent to Welns.io - a consumer-facing marketplace for discovering and booking beauty services. It appears to be an independent platform (not connected to MioSalon/Waffor) focused specifically on the South African market.
