# ViaGlamour Research Notes

## Overview
**Website:** https://viaglamour.com/
**Type:** White-label cosmetics manufacturing and dropshipping platform
**Location:** Toronto, Canada (cosmetics lab based in Toronto)
**Tagline:** "Create your own vegan cosmetics, skincare, and beauty products"

## Business Model

ViaGlamour operates as a **white-label cosmetics manufacturer and dropshipper** with three main service offerings:

### 1. Purchase Wholesale (Starting at $5)
Order products in bulk at wholesale prices and receive them at your business address for distribution.

### 2. Dropship Products
Customize ViaGlamour's existing products with your own designs and sell everywhere without buying inventory. ViaGlamour ships directly to your customers.

### 3. Design Services (Free Logos)
ViaGlamour provides design services to help brands create their product packaging and branding.
